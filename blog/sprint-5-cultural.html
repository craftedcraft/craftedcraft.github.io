Sprint 5 - Cultural Blog

Describe the impact the core learning has had on your Foundations learning journey.

The cultural and core learning curriculum has had a massive impact on my foundations learning journey, it has allowed me to better understand myself and how important EQ is and how it can completely change your outlook.

What are 1 - 3 new things you have learned about yourself as a result of the core learning?

These exercises have allowed me to learn a number of new things about myself and how I see others, some things I knew about myself but has allowed me to explore and understand them in more detail.

  1. I've learnt in more detail how I need to get into a state of focuss to maximise my efficiency, I've always explored this but I've learnt a few more tricks to ensure I'm aware when my attention is getting hijacked.
  2. I've learnt I'm a good listener but not a very good at articulating my thoughts.
  3. I've learnt that it's ok not to know everything and to be comfortable in not knowing because it is and area I'll experince a lot in this journey.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Personally the core learning curriculum has been most surprising in the fact of it's importance. It makes total sense but seems to be neglected by a number organisations.

What was the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

The most challenginng part for me was getting the core learning into words, I've always been quite visual and this is an onging challenge for me.

Any thoughts you want to share about your core learning journey in Foundations?

I feel these cultural and core skills are imensly important not only within the tech industry but in life in general so I'm glad they're prioritised.
